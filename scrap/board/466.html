Reinforce your cities in the Cyclades archipelago!

Attract more favors from the Gods with Cyclades: Monuments, a mini-expansion for Cyclades that consists of ten monument miniatures and ten associated monument cards. Now you can build temples dedicated to Zeus or Poseidon's glory, a great university to Athena, or a citadel from which Ares will watch down with each of these new buildings giving you a unique power to achieve victory!
