Community Wiki

Online Play

  • Sea3d and http://www.s3dconnector.net/ (real time play vs people only. No AI. Various options, including the desert and some volcano variant scenarios)

Contents (Mayfair 4th Edition):

  • 11 Terrain Hexes (1 Desert; 2 Fields; 2 Forest; 2 Pasture; 2 Mountains; 2 Hills)
  • 2 Frame Pieces with Harbors (1 Generic; 1 Wool)
  • 2 All-Sea Frame Pieces
  • 2 Sets of Wooden Playing Pieces:
    • 10 Settlements (5 x 2 Colors)
    • 8 Cities (4 x 2 Colors)
    • 30 Roads (15 x 2 Colors)
  • 25 Resource Cards (5 Lumber; 5 Grain; 5 Wool; 5 Brick; 5 Ore)
  • 9 Development Cards (6 Knight Cards; 1 Monopoly Card; 1 Year of Plenty Card; 1 Road Building Card)
  • 2 Blank Cards & 1 Blank Hex
  • 2 "Building Costs" Cards
  • 28 Circular Numbered Tokens
  • Rules

The 1999 Mayfair 3rd edition expansion (488) is virtually the same with the only difference being the 2 Ocean and 2 Harbour tiles are hexes rather than frame pieces.
